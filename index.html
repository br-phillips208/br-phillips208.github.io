About

Building what matters

I've spent my career at the intersection of people, process, and technology — leading teams that make customers feel seen and supported. Whether it's building a first enablement function from scratch, scaling a CX org, or rethinking onboarding from the ground up, I bring both the strategic vision and the hands-on grit to get it done.

My approach is grounded in empathy and driven by data. I care deeply about the humans I work with — the customers who trust us, the teams I lead, and the organizations I help grow. I believe the best outcomes happen when people are equipped, engaged, and genuinely cared for.

I'm based in Sandpoint, Idaho, where I spend my off-hours on mountain trails, with my family, and occasionally perfecting a roast profile.
